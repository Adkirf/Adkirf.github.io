2022 - 2023

Inspiration - Entering Web3.0

Since the end of 2021 I was enthusiastically exploring the Crypto- and Web3.0 Community. Especially in the NFT space I had big fun. Each project I joined or watched made me think how I would design or improve the UX, efficiency or other problems that needed to be solved.

2023 - January

The Choice - Getting started

After having played enough with the thought of learning to code, I used the new start of a year to make it real. I started to look for ressource to learn, books, tutorials, experienced people on social media AND joined the Alchemy Web3 University.

January - February

First Project - Adkirf Portfolio

Together with other peers of the Alchimey University I started to build my very first project: the Adkirf Portfolio! This serves not only to apply the basics I have learned so far, but also as the foundation on which new tools, skills and abilities will be applied.
